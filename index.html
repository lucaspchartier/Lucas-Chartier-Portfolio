About Me

My journey into web development.

  • May 2017

    Graduated from Lasell College

    I got my Bachelor of Science in entrepeneurship while working full-time at Lasell's on-campus college radio station, 102.9FM WLAS, and was promoted to station manager during my senior year.

  • May 2018

    Took online Digital Marketing Circuit course at General Assembly

    A year after receiving my bachelor's degree from Lasell, I started looking into General Assembly for courses as I continued to work at my gym and decided to take their 5-week online course in digital marketing.

  • June 2018

    Began teaching myself basic coding

    As I gradually opened up to General Assembly, I decided that it was time to make a change in my career, and wanted to further pursue web developemnt. I began using online resources to gradually teach myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

  • October 2018

    Took Web Development Immersive at General Assembly's Boston campus

    When the opportunity came, I reached out to General Assembly again, and was enrolled in their 3-month web development immersive program, where made 4 full-stack web applications as I began looking into web development opportunities.

Travel Blog

A full-stack application for users to log and store their hotel stays during their travels.

For this final capstone project, this full-stack application was constructed using Ruby on Rails for the API and ReactJS for the client. My main focus was on the UI and the styling to make it appear as more travel-focused and appealing to travel enthusiasts.

Travel Blog
  • Date: January 2019

Life and Dreams

A full-stack application for users to add goals they want to achieve in their lifetime and check them off upon completion.

For this group project, this full-stack application was constructed using Express for the API and HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery for the client. With 2 other developers, we had to logistically plan out which developer was working on which part of the API and client, as well as comparing and merging seperate pull requests on GitHub.

Life and Dreams
  • Date: January 2019

Music Database

A full-stack application for users to add artists of their choosing and individual releases under their artists.

For this individual project, this full-stack application was constructed using Ruby on Rails for the API and HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery for the client. While I didn't focus too much on the styling at first, I plan on really overhauling the styling once I complete my functioning release actions.
