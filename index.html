About Me

My journey into web development.

  • 2013-2017

    Attended Lasell College

    I got my Bachelor of Science in entrepeneurship while working full-time at our every own on-campus college radio station, 102.9FM WLAS Lasell College Radio, and was promoted to station manager during my senior year.

  • May 2018

    Began Digital Marketing Circuit course at General Assembly

    As I hit a rocky road during my sales career after graduating college, I started looking into General Assembly for courses while working part-time at my gym and decided to take their 5-week online course in digital marketing.

  • June 2018

    Began teaching myself basic coding and development skills

    After opening up to General Assembly, I decided that it was time to make a change in my life, and wanted to pursue a career in web developemnt. I began using online resources to gradually teach myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

  • October 2018

    Started Web Development Immersive at General Assembly's Boston campus

    When the opportunity came, I reached out to General Assembly again, and was enrolled in their 3-month web development immersive program, where made 4 full-stack web applications as I began looking into web development opportunities.
